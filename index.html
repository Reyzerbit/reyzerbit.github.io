Welcome

Hello! My name is Jacob, but known online as Reyzerbit. That name was something I made up as a kid, playing off of my middle name "Rey". When coming up with an online name for gaming and software freelancing, that popped into my head and I decided to go with it.

This webpage is meant to be the center of my online presence. Through this website, you can contact me, request commissions for freelance software jobs, and donate to help me keep developing!

As of right now, I am a junior at BYU Provo, studying as a  CompSci major. I'm married to the love of my life, Felicity, and am also an avid gamer.

I am fluent in Java and C++, with some experience in C#, HTML, and CSS. I am very willing and comfortable with learning new languages however, and am currently looking to expand my repertoire. 

If you would like me to join your team or project, feel free to shoot me a message. I love working on cross-platform applications, Minecraft mods and plugins, and creatign websites, and will willingly join any projects associated with those themes.
